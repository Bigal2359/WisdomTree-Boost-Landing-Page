ETPs from WisdomTree

WisdomTree offers tactical trading and hedging tools through our range of fully collateralised exchange traded products—Boost ETPs. Designed for sophisticated investors, Boost ETPs are available in unleveraged as well as 2x to 5x short and leveraged formats, across a variety of asset classes—equities, commodities, fixed income, currencies and alternatives.
